About This Project

This project was developed and is fully maintained by the Developer Team of the Aachen Investment Club. Our Portfolio Management Team is in charge of managing the portfolios that are being displayed in this website. For cost and performance reasons, we use a daily granularity. We compute all metrics and plots from scratch.
